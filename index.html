Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has successfully launched its newest satellite, a significant milestone in the country's efforts to strengthen its communication infrastructure and promote technological advancements. The satellite, named Bangabandhu-2, was launched from a space center in China and is expected to enhance connectivity, particularly in remote and underserved areas.

Paypal will Come soon in Bangladesh

Date: 07/01/2025

Time: 10.00 AM

In a major development for the country’s digital economy, PayPal has announced that it will soon be available in Bangladesh. The global online payment platform’s entry into the Bangladeshi market is expected to significantly boost e-commerce, international business transactions, and financial inclusion.
